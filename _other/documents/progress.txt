* Business Flow
    1) Adviser receives new Organization to onboard.
    2) First the Advisor must do a little preliminary research on Organization: business type, number of employees, etc.
       CC could perhaps assist with this (0% done).
    3) The Organization data is entered into CC's Organization Profile (100% done)
    4) The next step for the Advisor is to tweak the CIS Controls & Safeguards. Remove those which are irrelevant (100% done), combine others (100% done), rename some (0% done).
       To help with this task, CC can generate an initial starting point, by using the Organization Profile (0% done). Clicking on a button would apply the CC CIS template (0% done).
    5) The next step for the Advisor is to meet the customer (Organization) in a face-to-face meeting. The purpose of this meeting is to gather information for the GAP analysis.
       To aid the Advisor with this task, CC can generate a preliminary GAP aanalyse checklist (25% done). This is a printout (must be pretty & readable) or online (iPad) version of the active CIS Controls & Safeguards, with space for comments, etc.
    6) Having talked to the customer and gathered information for the GAP analysis, the Advisor can now refine the CIS Controls & Safeguards (100% done)
    7) Using this and other information CC can now generate a GAP analysis, during an interactive session with the Advisor (0% done)
    8) The next step is to activate the customer to get the compliance process going. For this purpose, first the Advisor creates a number of Profiles (100% done) for those responsible on the customer side. These Profiles do not have login access to CC.
    9) The Advisor can now generate Tasts individually on each CIS Safeguard (80% done), as well as Tasks not related to CIS (100%). The Advisor can also generate all Tasks related to a whole CIS Control (that is all the Safeguards) (0% done), or to all active Controls in one go (0% done).
    9) These Tasks can be connected to the Profiles (100% done), at which time Profile gets notified (0% done) that he has been assigned a Task.
    10) The rest is an ongoing process. The Profiles can upload Assets (25% done) to document Task completion status.
